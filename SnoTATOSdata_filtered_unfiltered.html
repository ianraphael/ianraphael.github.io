SnoTATOS live data

We deployed four SnoTATOS networks in the Lincoln Sea in late April 2024 as part of the NASA ARCSIX project. Below you'll find live data from the active networks.

Data are collected with a four-hour sampling interval. We forward-fill each station's time series to remove gaps, then low-pass-filter the data using an exponential moving average filter. The ultrasonic rangefinders are sensitive to icing and wind-blown snow; for this reason, readings are typically noisy during the winter.

Note that snow depth has not been recalibrated after melt season, so while the traces after ~September 1 reflect snow accumulation, they do not indicate true snow depth.
